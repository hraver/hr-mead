About Hudson River Mead Company

Making mead began as a trial to see if it was something that we would like to drink. It was! Although the process of making mead is very similar to that of making wine, the drink is uniquely different.The flavors we made were so good that we wanted everyone to enjoy them.

This resulted in the formation of Hudson River Mead Company. Hudson River Mead Company is located in the historic Village of Coxsackie NY, right on the Hudson River.

We produce only small batches at a time. This ensures good quality control.
