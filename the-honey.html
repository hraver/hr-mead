The Honey

Our mead is made only from local honey. Bella's Bees Honey is owned and operated by Mark and Lisa Gerrain of Coxsackie, NY. They have proven to be reliable source of organic, flavorful raw honey. Different pollen sources, such as clover, wildflower, orange blossom, etc., will create differing honey flavors, and will affect the flavor of the mead.

Save The Bees!

Bees do much more than make honey for our delicious mead!

Please stop using pesticides in your yard. The population of bees is dwindling. Also, plant a lot of flowers that attract bees that bloom throughout the Spring, Summer and Fall. Bees require a lot of pollen! Put out shallow water dishes in the garden with rocks and sticks in them so bees can rest and have a drink. If you can, get involved with bee keeping, or support local bee keepers in your area. And, always spread the word to let others know how to protect bees, too.
